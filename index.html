Livermorium


What is livermorium? and some facts!

Livermorium is highly radioactive metal, of which only a few atoms have ever been made. It was discovered by Scientists from the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research in Dubna, Russia and the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, California, USA. Currently it has no uses and are only used in research
